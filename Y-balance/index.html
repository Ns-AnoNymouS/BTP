Y Balance Test

Follow these steps carefully before starting the test

1

Check your camera

Make sure your camera is working, and you are fully visible with enough space in front of you to draw the Y on the ground.

2

Mark the Y center

Place a book or object on the ground as a reference. Use both index fingertips together to mark the center point, then mark the other two points for the Y arms.

3

Calibrate with a card

4

Perform the test

Stand with one toe at the Y center and stretch your other toe forward along each Y direction (Anterior, Posteromedial, Posterolateral) as far as possible.
